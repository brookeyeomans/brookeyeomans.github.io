What is Paella?

A festive occasion calls for a big statement, and the paella is just that: a colorful rice dish bursting with clams,
mussels and shrimp along with smoky chorizo and saffron for tons of flavor. You don’t need much else to serve
alongside it, but you could make a green salad if you feel inspired. Set the whole pan of paella on your picnic table,
and bring out some crispy bread and wine glasses. Summer is too short not to celebrate it with friends!

Types of Paella

Paella comes in traditional, meat, seafood, and vegetarian varieties. What makes
paella truly special is its adaptability to the ingredients on hand when families
gather, allowing it to become a delicious reflection of the ingredients and
flavors that each family can bring to the table.
