Valencia, Spain

Valencia, a captivating city on Spain's eastern coast, is renowned as the birthplace of paella.
The city's culinary history is deeply rooted in this iconic dish. Valencia's cuisine is a delightful
blend of Moorish, Mediterranean, and Spanish influences, with a strong emphasis on paella,
featuring fresh seafood and seasonal ingredients. It's a place where the tradition of paella is
alive and thriving, a testament to the region's love for this iconic Spanish dish.
